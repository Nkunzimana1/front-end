About Us

About Intama Business Group

Intama Business Group is a dynamic Rwandan company dedicated to providing quality solutions across multiple sectors: technology (computers & accessories), design & construction consultancy, agriculture and farming, and event management. Founded on a passion for innovation and service excellence, we offer trusted products and professional services that meet the needs of individuals, businesses, and communities.

Our History

Intama Business Group was established in 2023 by Mr. Eng. IRAMBONA Aime, starting as a small business specializing in selling computers and accessories. Due to the founder's vision and the growing demand, the company expanded into design & construction, agriculture & farming, and professional event management services.
