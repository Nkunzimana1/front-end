Mission

Intama Business Group is committed to providing secure, rel iable, and sustainable services in computer & accessories sales, design and construction consultancy, modern agriculture, and professional event management. We strive to: Empower communities with digital and agricultural solutions. Promote quality construction through innovative designs. Deliver memorable and well-managed events. Maintain customer satisfaction and long-term relationships through trust and service excellence.

Value

Innovation We embrace new ideas and technology in everything we do. Integrity We work with honesty, professionalism, and transparency. Vision

To become a leading, innovative, and trusted multi- sector enterprise in Rwanda and beyond delivering high-quality solutions in technology, construction, agriculture, and event management.

About Intama Business Group

Intama Business Group is a dynamic Rwandan company dedicated to providing quality solutions across multiple sectors: technology (computers & accessories), design & construction consultancy, agriculture and farming, and event management. Founded on a passion for innovation and service excellence, we offer trusted products and professional services that meet the needs of individuals, businesses, and communities.

Our History

Intama Business Group was established in 2023 by Mr. Eng. IRAMBONA Aime, starting as a small business specializing in selling computers and accessories. Due to the founder's vision and the growing demand, the company expanded into design & construction, agriculture & farming, and professional event management services.
